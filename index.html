About Me (CV)

I'm a software engineer at Snowflake.

I obtained my PhD at Department of Computer Science, University of California Irvine in December, 2020. My PhD dissertation is about optimizing LSM storage for Big Data Management Systems (BDMSs) under supervision of Professor Michael J. Carey. Before that, I obtained my Master's degree in Software Engineering from School of Software, Tsinghua University in July, 2016, and my bachelor's degreee in Software Engineering from School of Software, Tongji University in July, 2013.
